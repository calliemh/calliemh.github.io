Great Gastronomy at UNCG - Where to Eat on Tate Street?
Like these picks? Try creating a Meal Plan

New to UNCG and ready to expand your palette?

In your second or third year and tired of packing your lunches every day?

Visiting your indecisive highschool friend who goes here and have to pick where to eat?

Worry not, UNCG is home to an abundance of cullinary options! In this article, we'll take you through some of our best spots to grab some grub. And the best part?
They're all on the same street!

Coffeeology

If you're looking for a quiet place to have a cup of coffee or a delicious meal, this coffee shop might be just what you're looking for! Get your schoolwork done in their clean and professional environment with some espresso or one of their carefully crafted lattes!

Tate Street Coffee House

Being a part of Greensboro's coffee scene since 1993, this lively coffee house prides itself in organic fair-trade coffees.
And if live music is your thing, stop by for live jazz Thursday nights and mornings Sat and Sun, or Sunday afternoon Irish music.

Gong Cha

Are you a fan of specialty teas? Then stop by this more recent addition to Tate Street's cullinary collection. Gong Cha is a Taiwanese bubble tea franchise that has more recently opened a location on our very own Tate Street! Stop by for a milk tea or a cool and refreshing fruit tea whenever you get the chance!
